Game 2: Fault Finding in Abnormal Scenarios

Problem Description: A group designed "pet-approaching feeding" by adding a separate "sound sensor" to detect pet sounds and then control food delivery. Does this conform to the identified patterns?

Correct Explanation:

It violates the common requirement of using a weight sensor to confirm food amount. There is no need to add a new sound sensor—existing patterns show that weight detection is sufficient for all scenarios, and additional sensors are unnecessary.
